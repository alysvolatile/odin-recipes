Potatoes: God's Mistake?

When will someone think of the children???

Everyone thinks that potatoes are just so lovely. That they're the backbone of many delicious recipes. That they are a wonderful source of easy calories for very, very cheap. And these aren't wrong. But. BUT.

But isn't it just all too suspicious that these ground apples just showed up one day? Aren't we going to talk about the real truth here? The truth that aliens brought them here. I'm tired of being silenced by the Potato Illuminati!

But until that day, please enjoy this recipe for delicious mashed potatoes, suitable for vegans.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs russet potatos, peeled and cut into 1 1/2 inch chunks
  • 1/3 C whole unroasted cashews. (If you don't have a high speed blender, no worries, just boil them for 20 minutes beforehand or soak overnight and drain.)
  • 1/2 C veggie broth at room temperature
  • 1/3 C refined coconut oil at room temperature
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • Lots of fresh black pepper - though as in the case of the Orzilla<, the stale stuff in the pantry works fine as well
